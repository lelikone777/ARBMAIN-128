Who is coming to clean ?

Professionally trained cleaners will come to clean your apartment - Russian girls, aged from 24 to 40 years.

All cleaners must first be certified in our training center, as well as pass a security check, before becoming a member of the СleanBros team.

All cleaners wear company-issued uniforms with the CleanBros logo.

Cleaning is done with professional technical and chemical products brought by our cleaners:

  • Mop
  • Microfiber cloths
  • window cleaning wipes
  • Sponges
  • Cleaning brushes
  • Squeegee
  • Garbage bags

Kiehl's eco-friendly cleaning products:

  • Grease remover
  • Disinfectant
  • Universal remedy
  • Window cleaner

We bring with a compact professional Soteco vacuum cleaner, as well as an iron, a cleaning bucket, a steam generator, a dry cleaner and more AT YOUR REQUEST.
